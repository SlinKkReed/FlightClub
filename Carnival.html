Spring Fest 2022 Flight 1876

Games, Attractions, Food

Rock climbing, test your strength hammer game, obstacle courses, multiple bounce houses and many more!

If you're hungry for food there will be plenty of food trucks on the lawn, some even free. You have options of seafood, mexican, wings, barbeque etc. To finish it off, enjoy a cool snowcone from Bahama Bucks!

Even if you want to just come vibe and listen to music, this is the place to be on Friday 4/12!

FREE! Prizes/Giveaways

There can not be a PV event without giving back to the students. Everyone loves crocs and you do not want to miss out on FREE spring fest croc charms!

If that isn't enough there will also be FREE 2022 flight 1876 spring fest t-shirts all provided by CAB! Who knows what other prizes you may get if you attend!

This event is about bringing the carnival to campus. It will allow the student body to unwind, meet, and network with others. It gives those students a chance to get out and take a mental break from studying for finals.
